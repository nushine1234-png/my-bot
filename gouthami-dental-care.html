Our Mission

Gouthami Dental Care was founded with a singular purpose — to bridge the gap between world-class dentistry and underserved communities. As the rural development program of Nu Shine Dental, we bring fully equipped mobile dental units, experienced doctors, and modern treatments right to the doorsteps of those who need them most.

We believe that a healthy smile is not a luxury — it is a fundamental right. Through regular free checkup camps, awareness programs, and subsidized treatments, Gouthami Dental Care is working tirelessly to ensure that geography never stands between a person and their smile.

In the coming years, we plan to expand our reach to every major rural area and city across Andhra Pradesh, creating a statewide network of accessible, compassionate dental care.

Services We Provide

Through our mobile camps and outreach centres, we offer a wide range of dental services.

🦷

Dental Checkups & Cleaning

Comprehensive oral examinations and professional cleaning at no or minimal cost during our camps.

🩺

Extractions & Fillings

Basic restorative procedures including tooth extractions, cavity fillings, and preventive treatments.

🦷

Root Canal Treatment

Advanced RCT services at subsidised rates to save infected teeth and relieve pain.

👶

Child Dental Care

Special focus on paediatric dentistry — educating children and parents on proper oral hygiene habits.

📢

Awareness Programs

Community workshops on oral hygiene, nutrition, and preventive care for all age groups.

🚐

Mobile Dental Clinic

Fully equipped mobile dental units that travel to remote villages, bringing the clinic to the community.

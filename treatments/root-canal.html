Root Canal Treatment

Pain-free root canal to save your natural tooth

Save Your Tooth with Root Canal Therapy

Root canal treatment is a dental procedure used to treat infection at the centre of a tooth. Contrary to popular belief, modern root canal treatments are virtually painless and can save your natural tooth from extraction.

At Nu Shine Dental, we use advanced rotary endodontic technology and precise anesthesia techniques to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure.

Benefits

Saves natural tooth
Pain-free procedure
Prevents infection spread
Restores chewing function

Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful?

With modern anesthesia and techniques, root canal treatment is no more painful than getting a filling. Most patients report being comfortable throughout.

How many visits does it take?

Most root canals are completed in 1-2 visits, depending on the complexity of the infection.

How long does a root canal last?

With proper care and a dental crown, a root canal-treated tooth can last a lifetime.

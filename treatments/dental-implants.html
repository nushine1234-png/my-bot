Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement that looks and feels natural

Restore Your Smile with Dental Implants

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. A titanium post is surgically placed into the jawbone, acting as an artificial root, and topped with a natural-looking crown.

At Nu Shine Dental, we use CBCT 3D imaging for precise implant planning and placement, ensuring optimal results with minimal discomfort.

Benefits

Permanent solution
Natural look & feel
Preserves jawbone
Full chewing function

Types of Implants

Single Implant

Replace a single missing tooth with one implant and crown.

Multiple Implants

Bridge-supported implants for multiple missing teeth.

Full Mouth Implants

All-on-4 or all-on-6 implants for complete arch restoration.
